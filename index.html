Top 5 biggest sports leagues in the world

Globally, the sports industry has been estimated to be worth over $620 billion. This indicates, that professional games are big business in today’s world with money being generated from different sources. Check Top 5 Richest Sports Leagues In The World Right Now.

National Football League

The National Football League is an American Football League. It is the highest professional level of American football in the world. Miami Dolphins, Houston Texans, and Los Angeles Chargers are some of the famous teams. It is a far more physical game than association football and thus requires lots of protective gear and equipment. In 2021, the league earned $16 Billion and the amount is only expected to rise. Thus the National Football League is the world’s richest and biggest sports league.

Indian Premier League

This is the most pleasant surprise any Indian and especially an IPL lover can get. Indian Premier League, the brainchild of BCCI, occupies 2nd place in the list of the biggest and richest sports leagues in the world. This is incredible, especially when cricket is a major sport only in a handful of countries, and out of those, only 3-4 are dominating. This becomes even more fascinating when people in Europe or America are not at all aware of cricket. Recently, Viacom18 has bagged the digital streaming rights of IPL at a whopping Rs 48390 crore for 5 years. IPL makes a revenue of $10 billion in 2022.

Major League Baseball

Again the American league occupies one of the top spots in list of biggest sports leagues. Major League Baseball is a baseball league that is considered the oldest major professional sports league in the world. As cricket is famous in India, Baseball is famous in America. If you remember, the only All-Star cricket tournament took place in America on the baseball ground itself. The league produces $10 Billion per annum which makes it 3rd richest sports league in the world.

National Basketball Association

NBA or National Basketball Association is a professional basketball league in North America. The NBA is the world’s most prestigious and consists of the richest players across the globe. Players like Lebron James, Anthony, and Butler are global icons in the Sports Industry. Teams like Minneapolis/Los Angeles Lakers, Miami Heat, and Boston Celtics have a worldwide fan base. The NBA generates $8 billion yearly as their revenue.

English Premier League

The English Premier League or EPL is considered the world’s most difficult football league. Unlike Serie A and Bundesliga, in EPL no club can guarantee success. Even a club that is at the top of the table for most of the season, can be found in lower places at the end of the season. Here the table positions keep on changing and so do the champions each season. The clubs have been owned by millionaires and billionaires. Fans do not have any control over the club, as fans in Germany have over their different clubs. The clubs in EPL, especially the big ones, are always under scrutiny for their expenditure. Well, quite clearly the league has huge revenue, to be precise $5.3 billion each year.
